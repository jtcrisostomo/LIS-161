Mademoiselle Caroline Riviére

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Riviére was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres, and
today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of the three portraits of the Riviére family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm
Home: Musée du Louvre, Paris
